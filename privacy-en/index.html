ZIP ONE Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AT GEAR WORKS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles user data in the mobile application “ZIP ONE” (“the App”).

1. Information we collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or location data.

The App does not transmit or store the contents of files or folders that you choose to compress or encrypt on any server operated by us or by third parties.

2. Data processing

All file compression and AES-256 encryption performed by the App take place entirely on your device.

We do not access, store, or analyze the contents of your files or the passwords you use within the App.

3. Third-party services

The App does not integrate with analytics tools, advertising networks, cloud services, or any other third-party services.

4. Sharing of data

We do not share any user data or information related to files processed by the App with third parties.

5. Security

The App is designed so that all processing is performed locally on your device, and it does not send your data over the network.

However, any backup, cloud sync, or other features provided by your device or operating system are outside the scope of this policy.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
