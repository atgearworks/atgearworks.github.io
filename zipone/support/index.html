Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I create password-protected ZIP files?
A. Yes. ZIP ONE supports creating password-protected ZIP files using AES‑256 encryption.

Q. Where are the ZIP files saved?
A. After creating a ZIP file, the share sheet will appear. By selecting “Save to Files,” you can save the ZIP file to any folder in iCloud Drive or on your device. ZIP ONE does not automatically save files; instead, you can freely choose the save location through the share sheet.

Q. What should I do if the app does not work properly?
A. Please try restarting the app or your device. If the issue persists, contact us via the support email.
